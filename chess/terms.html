    Section I - Rules in Game

  1. Standard Chess rules apply
  2. Unsportsmanlike conduct and cheating and prohibited
    1. Such conduct may be penalised through score deductions or forfieture or both
  3. There is no time limit imposed on games, other than that imposed by the school bell, or both players
  4. Any student is encouraged to attend, either to spectate or to play.
  5. If a game is forced to end by lack of time, and neither player resigns, then the game is to be decided as follows:
    For each player, add up the pieces of the opponent that they have took and assign them a score as so:
    Pawns - x
    Rooks & Bishops - x
    Queen - x
    Whichever player has the highest score wins
    Note: Numbers subject to change, pending confirmation with Mrs Doran

    Section II - Leaderboard

  1. Any student is freely permitted to join the leaderboard with an initial score of 100
  2. A score below 100 is not permitted
  3. Changes to the score shall be determined by the Elo Rating system, as implemented
  4. Students will be expected to register games played
  5. Attempting to fraudulently gain points will result in point deductions being applied
    On any first violation, the action is to be reversed and points returned as though they never happened
    On any second violation, the player's points are to be reduce by half
    On any third violation, the player's points are to be reset to 100
    Any fourth violation results in an immediate four week ban
    Any fifth violation results in a permenant ban
    'Ban' is to be interpretted as a removal from the leaderboard and prohibition on games counted towards it
  6. Tournament Moderators have discretion in the application of the above Act
  7. No show policy: Members that are not present for three consequtive weeks will have their score reduced by 40 for each week until reset to 100
